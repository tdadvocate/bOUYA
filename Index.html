Welcome

Welcome to our site! We hope that you enjoy your time on the site. Our goal with this site was to create a one-stop shop for all history and info we could find on the OUYA consoles' hardware and software as well as some other features throughout the site. Majority of the navigation through this site is done through the menu drop down at the top of the page for ease of access and simplicity.
